For leads who've been presented to and are ready for, or need nudging toward, the physician teleconsultation — the step before treatment planning and closing.

Goal: Confirm a teleconsult date
Ready to move

Direct booking follow-up

Engaged well in the presentation, few remaining objections

Hi [First Name], Thank you for your time during the presentation — based on what we discussed, I think a teleconsultation with our physician is the right next step. This is where your specific case is reviewed and a personalized plan is discussed. I can offer [Date/Time] or [Date/Time] for a 20–30 minute video consultation. Which works best for you? Looking forward to it, [Your Name] Revivee Ventures Inc.

Doctor-access follow-up

Subject: Your questions deserve a direct answer from our physician

This way you get medically sound answers specific to your case. Would [Date/Time] or [Date/Time] work for a video call with the doctor? Best, [Your Name] Revivee Ventures Inc.
